Know Mohan Biscuit Factory

Mohan Biscuit Factory was started by Lt.Purna Bahadur Shrestha in 2011 BS. At the beginning, there were only 5 staffs. Khaja Biscuit and Khasta Biscuits were the only products produced at that starting phase of the factory.

How Mohan Biscuit Factory Started

Situated at Dhankuta, Mohan Biscuit Factory now serves most of the eastern part of Nepal. Around 12 people have been employeed in the factory. At present, factory produces around 16 different types of hard and soft dough products. We have learned from our experiences and come up with many develpoments in our working system. Along with the old conventional fire oven, we have also introduced modern machines and oven in our factory.
